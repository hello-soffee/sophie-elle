About

I’m Sophie, a Montreal-based creative.

Growing up, Art class was always my favourite. In summer camp, I advocated for arts & crafts over competitive sports (having to participate in sports made me cry). I’ve always been a daydreamer and felt most like myself when I was exercising my imagination.

I’m always learning and creating; it’s been a lifelong loop. In between my years of education in communications and the arts, and working in people-first environments, I picked up a digital + film camera and dove into the Adobe Creative Suite + UX/UI design.

I turned thirty and noticed the magic in my creativity. It felt intuitive to (finally) merge work and play. This led me to establish myself as a creative boutique offering photography and graphic design. Think of me as your creative partner; let’s create something with hearts in it.

Warmly, Sophie
